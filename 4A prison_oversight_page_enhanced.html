OVERSIGHT CRISIS

The Critical Need for Independent Oversight

Arizona's criminal legal system faces profound challenges that demand urgent attention and systematic reform through independent monitoring and accountability.

Zero Independent Oversight

With zero independent oversight bodies monitoring Arizona's state prisons, transparency and accountability are critically lacking, allowing harmful conditions to persist unchecked.

0 Oversight Bodies

Taxpayer Cost Crisis

This absence of oversight contributes to a corrections system that costs Arizona taxpayers over $1.5 billion annually while failing to meet basic standards of effectiveness.

$1.5B Annual Cost

Constitutional Violations

The ongoing Jensen v. Shinn lawsuit demonstrates the urgent need for independent oversight, with 34,500+ people in ADCRR experiencing constitutional violations.

34,500+ People Affected
