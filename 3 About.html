Our Story

From personal experience to policy change - the journey of Praxis Initiative

2018

Foundation

Praxis Initiative was founded by directly impacted individuals who experienced the failures of Arizona's criminal legal system firsthand. Our founders recognized the urgent need for independent oversight and evidence-based reform.

2019-2021

Building Capacity

We developed our core programs, built partnerships with community organizations, and began advocating for policy reforms. Our team grew to include experts in policy, advocacy, and community organizing.

2022-2023

Legislative Advocacy

We intensified our legislative efforts, working with bipartisan coalitions to advance prison oversight legislation. Our advocacy reached new heights with comprehensive policy proposals.

2024

Historic Victory

After years of advocacy, we achieved a historic victory with the passage of legislation establishing the Independent Correctional Oversight Office in Arizona - a first for the state.

2025

Expanding Impact

Building on our success, we're expanding our programs and continuing to advocate for comprehensive criminal legal system reform across Arizona and beyond.

Our Guiding Principles

The values that drive our work and shape our approach to criminal legal system reform

Lived Experience Leadership

Those most impacted by the system should lead the efforts to change it. Our directly impacted-led approach ensures authentic representation and effective solutions.

Evidence-Based Advocacy

We ground our work in rigorous research, data analysis, and proven best practices to ensure our advocacy efforts are effective and sustainable.

Bipartisan Collaboration

Criminal justice reform transcends political boundaries. We work across the aisle to build coalitions and find common ground for meaningful change.

Transparency & Accountability

We believe in open government and public accountability. Our work promotes transparency in corrections and holds institutions accountable to the public.

Community-Centered Approach

Real change happens at the community level. We prioritize community engagement, education, and empowerment in all our initiatives.

Restorative Justice

We advocate for approaches that focus on healing, rehabilitation, and successful reintegration rather than purely punitive measures.
